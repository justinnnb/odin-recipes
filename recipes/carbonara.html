Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a very simple pasta-based dish that came from Italy. This recipe contains only 6 ingredients which makes it easy to make at home.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Paremsan
  • Olive Oil
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Bacon
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Put olive oil in the pan and cook the bacon on low heat until grease comes out.
  2. Beat 1 whole egg and 2 egg yolks into a bowl.
  3. Put parmesan, and salt and pepper to test.
